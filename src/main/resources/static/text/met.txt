Located on the border of Central Park on Museum Mile, the Metropolitan Museum is one of the largest and most comprehensive art museums in the world. 
With over two million works of art spanning 5,000 years, the Met presents the best of human creativity from around the globe. 
From the splendors of ancient Egypt, to the spectacular New American Wing, to the Met`s beloved Impressionist paintings, a world of great art awaits you at NYC`s most-visited attraction. 
The collection and exhibitions are free with Museum admission. 
Plus, enjoy dining, the Audio Guide, and shopping at the Met Store.
